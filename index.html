WELCOME to Beanz!

Welcome to Beanz Coffee Company! Founded in 1919, our roasting processes has been perfected to provide a perfect cup with guaranteed freshness. Beanz Coffee Company selects only the best beanz from independent growers all over the world. Our beanz are hand-picked, and roasted in our facility attached to our coffee shop. The atmosphere is relaxed and inviting, and our friendly staff are ready to serve.

Beanz Coffee is the best in California! You won't be disappointed in the selection or the flavor! We can't be beat, so come on in and try your taste at the world's best coffees - roasted fresh, daily!
